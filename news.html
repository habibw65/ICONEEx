ICONEEx Project Kicks Off!

April 2023

We are excited to announce the official launch of the ICONEEx project, aimed at developing a satellite-based NEE upscaling deep learning model using ERA5 data. Stay tuned for updates!

First Data Collection Phase Completed

September 15, 2025

The initial phase of data collection from our five training towers has been successfully completed. This data will be crucial for the model development.

New Publication on Satellite-based NEE Modeling

October 1, 2025

Our team has published a new paper on the methodologies for satellite-based Net Ecosystem Exchange modeling. Find it in the Outputs section soon!

Upcoming Workshop on GHG Monitoring

November 5, 2025

Join us for an upcoming workshop focusing on advanced techniques for greenhouse gas monitoring and modeling. Details to be announced.
